Foreclosure Trend Over Time

This bar chart illustrates the number of foreclosed properties in the Louisville Metro area from 2014 to 2024, highlighting years with notably high foreclosure activity. A 2020-2021 housing report notes that Foreclosure rates gradually declined in 2020 and remained low throughout 2021, largely due to federal protections established by the CARES Act. Louisville consistently ranks among the U.S. metro areas with populations exceeding 1 million that have the highest foreclosure rates.
